Additional Attributes

Both <audio> and <video> tags support additional attributes like:

  • autoplay: Starts playing the media automatically.
  • loop: Loops the media.
  • muted: Mutes the media.

Conclusion

Embedding multimedia enhances the user experience on web pages. Use the <audio> and <video> tags to include audio and video content effectively.
